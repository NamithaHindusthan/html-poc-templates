5. 5️⃣ Notifications / Pop-up Design

Top-right corner for all dashboards

Dropdown / scrollable list

Real-time updates (via WebSocket or polling)

Example content:

“Policy #12345 expires in 7 days”

“New client assigned to you”

“Payment of $500 completed”

Click → navigates to relevant page (policy, client, payment)
