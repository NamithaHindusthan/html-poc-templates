### Thailand Off-Season Budget Plan (June–October)

**Duration:** 4 Nights, 5 Days

**Total Budget:** ₹40,000 per person (All inclusive)

---

## 1. Flights (₹25,000–₹28,000)

* **Airlines:** Budget carriers (Air India, Thai Airways, Scoot)
* **Booking Tips:** Use Skyscanner/Google Flights, book 2–3 months in advance
* **Estimated Cost:** ₹25,000–₹28,000 round trip

## 2. Accommodation (₹6,000–₹8,000)

* **Options:** Budget hotels, guesthouses, hostels
* **Destinations:** Bangkok, Pattaya
* **Booking Platforms:** Booking.com, Agoda
* **Estimated Cost:** ₹1,500–₹2,000 per night

## 3. Meals (₹2,800–₹3,500)

* **Street Food:** Affordable and delicious
* **Daily Budget:** ₹600–₹700 for three meals
* **Popular Dishes:** Pad Thai, Som Tum, Mango Sticky Rice

## 4. Transportation (₹2,000–₹2,500)

* **Within Cities:** BTS in Bangkok, tuk-tuks, Grab
* **Between Cities:** Buses or budget flights
* **Estimated Cost:** ₹2,000–₹2,500

## 5. Activities & Sightseeing (₹3,500–₹4,000)

* **Bangkok:** Grand Palace, Wat Pho, local markets
* **Pattaya:** Coral Island, Nong Nooch Tropical Garden, Sanctuary of Truth
* **Estimated Cost:** ₹3,500–₹4,000

## 6. Visa & TDAC (₹3,000–₹4,000)

* **Visa on Arrival:** Up to 15 days for Indian passport holders
* **TDAC:** Thailand Digital Arrival Card
* **Estimated Cost:** ₹3,000–₹4,000

---

## Sample Itinerary

**Day 1:** Arrival in Bangkok, check-in, explore markets, street food

**Day 2:** Bangkok sightseeing - Grand Palace, Wat Pho, evening river cruise

**Day 3:** Travel to Pattaya, check-in, relax

**Day 4:** Pattaya - Coral Island, Nong Nooch Garden, Sanctuary of Truth

**Day 5:** Return to Bangkok, last-minute shopping, departure

---

## Travel Tips

* **Weather:** Occasional rain, pack accordingly
* **Safety:** Thailand is generally safe; exercise standard precautions
* **Connectivity:** Local SIM card recommended
* **Language:** Basic English widely understood in tourist areas
* **Budget Tip:** Book flights and hotels early for best rates
